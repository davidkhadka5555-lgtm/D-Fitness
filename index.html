About David-Fitness

Founded in 2015, David-Fitness has been the premier destination for fitness enthusiasts in the community. Our mission is to empower individuals to lead healthier, more active lifestyles through expert guidance, state-of-the-art facilities, and a supportive community environment.

We believe that fitness is not just about physical transformation, but also about building mental strength, fostering lasting habits, and creating connections that last a lifetime. Join us and become part of the David-Fitness family!

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our gym, memberships, and services.

What are your gym hours? +

Our gym is open 24/7 for all members. The staff is available Monday-Friday 6 AM - 10 PM and Saturday-Sunday 8 AM - 6 PM for assistance.

Can I freeze my membership? +

Yes! You can freeze your membership for up to 3 months at no additional cost. Contact our front desk or call us for details.

Do you offer personal training? +

Yes! Premium members get 1 free session per month, VIP members get 4 sessions monthly, and additional sessions are available for purchase at $50/session.

What's your cancellation policy? +

You can cancel anytime with 30 days notice. No cancellation fees or hidden charges. Just contact our support team.

Do you have options for beginners? +

Absolutely! Our "Beginner's Guide" class runs every Monday and Wednesday at 9 AM. All trainers are certified to help beginners with proper form and progression.

Is there a free trial? +

Yes! New members get a 7-day free trial to experience our facilities, classes, and community. Just sign up online or visit us in person.
